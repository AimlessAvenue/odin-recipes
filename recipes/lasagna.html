Lasagna

Description

The tradition of lasagna originated in Emilia Romagna, and from there it has become one of the symbols of Italian cuisine throughout the world. Abroad, lasagne alla bolognese, made with bechamel and meat sauce, is well known. The Neapolitan version of this recipe includes among its ingredients tomato sauce, mozzarella, meatballs and Roman ricotta cheese. In Liguria, pesto is used as a sauce, while in Venice the sauce is replaced with red radicchio. The lasagna you eat depends on where you are.

ingredients for a 22cm baking tray (6 people)

For the puff pastry:

  • 150g Wheat flour type '00
  • 50g Wheat type '0
  • 2 Large whole eggs
  • Salt as needed
  • 250g of Parmesan for the layers

For the Bolognese sauce:

  • 600g Ground beef (not too lean)
  • 250g Minced pork (not too lean)
  • 700ml Thick tomato sauce + water
  • 1 Small white onion
  • 70g Celery
  • 70g Carrots
  • 3 tablespoons Extra virgin olive oil
  • 100ml Red wine (actually, white is good too)
  • 50ml Whole milk
  • Salt as needed
  • Black pepper as needed

For the Bechamel:

  • 500ml of whole milk, skimmed or vegetable. Or vegetable broth. Choose at your convenience.
  • 50g Butter (or 50 grams of extra virgin olive oil)
  • 50g Flour
  • Salt as needed
  • Black pepper as needed

Steps

  1. First of all, make the Bolognese sauce
  2. Then make the puff pastry. I recommend that you carve the rectangles directly to the size of your pan.
  3. Finally, prepare the Bechamel by mixing butter or oil with the flour on the heat, slowly adding milk and stirring constantly until the mixture is soft and smooth. Finally, add salt and flavor. Cover with a contact film.
  4. Once the bases are ready, nice and cold, you can assemble your lasagna.
  5. Add a ladle of rag첫 to a pan and then one or more sheets for lasagna to cover the base.
  6. Then add two ladles of meat sauce and a few spoonfuls of bechamel, finally add a sprinkling of Parmesan.
  7. Now add a new sheet of lasagna and continue following the order, from step 5.
  8. For the last layer, cover with two ladles of meat sauce, a few tablespoons of bechamel and finally a sprinkle of Parmesan.
  9. Finally cover the pan with aluminum foil, in this way the ingredients will blend perfectly without burning immediately.
  10. Bake in a static oven, hot at 180 째 in the central part, for about 30 minutes.

    11. The trick:Remember that the trick is the temperature, which Must be 180째. Ideally, the oven should be preheated,and if you have the option to choose various settings, heat the central part of the oven.

  11. After the indicated time, remove the aluminum foil, transfer the tray to the upper level and cook for another 15 minutes.
  12. Optional:The last 5 minutes turn on the grill and raise to 200 째 to achieve the mighty crust!
  13. Remove from the oven and leave to cool for at least 15 minutes.
  14. Enojoy your lasagna!

Tips for storage:

  • You can keep cooked in the fridge for 2 - 3 days, reheat at the moment or freeze!
  • Alternatively, you can prepare your lasagna well in advance and keep it in the fridge until ready to cook!
